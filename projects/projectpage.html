Hey Neighbor Brand
2020 — Personal Project, Design System, Logo, Motion, Interaction
Hey Neighbor is a platform that shares insider details about NYC apartments, ensuring NYC renters find comfortable and safe living conditions.

A big challenge with this project was how the platform itself focused on serving data. A focus on data can often make an experience dry. To ensure the platform felt friendly and welcoming, it was essential to portray warmness and humanity through the name, illustrations, and branding.
The Hey Neighbor concept and platform was created by myself and executed by a dev partner. We launched a V.1 of the platform but COVID circumstances eventually forced us to discontinue the project.

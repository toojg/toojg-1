Ar Welcome Box
2021 — Augmented Reality, Motion, Print Design
Sunrun is the largest residential solar company in the U.S. One reason for their growth is the focus they have on their sales team. They work hard to have their team feel motivated and taken care of.
‍
Sunrun asked us to create a “Welcome” box for their new sales hires that would contain all of the sales essentials—company swag, IDs, etc.
‍
I suggested we build an AR experience along with the design of the box. I didn’t love where we landed with the design of the box, however it was exciting to see the implementation of the AR experience.

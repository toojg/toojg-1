I was born and raised in a small town in southern Utah where I spent my childhood working in my Dad's cabinet shop. It was there I realized I loved creating and building things.

In high school, I started a band that needed merch and posters. This brought me to design. I’ve been obsessed with it ever since.

After spending a couple of years working with startups and screen printing posters, I moved to NYC to study under some of my design heroes. I loved learning and working there. Are you still reading this?

Eventually, Utah’s mountains called me back. Provo, Utah is where I currently reside, in a house that was built in 1885 that I renovated (with a lot of help from family).

When I’m not working, you can find me spending time with my family, listening to music or watching/playing basketball.

(I’m looking for more local pick-up basketball games, let me know if you hear of any.)

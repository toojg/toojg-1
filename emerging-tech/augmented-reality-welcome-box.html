Background

Sunrun is the largest residential solar company in the U.S. One reason for their growth is the focus they have on their sales team. They work hard to have their team feel motivated and taken care of.

Sunrun asked us to elevate the experience of new sales people coming onto the team.

Although Sunrun has a world-class building where they prepare their sales team for their work, not all salespeople could travel to Utah. As a result, we proposed creating an augmented reality (AR) experience and a custom-designed box that would include all the necessary sales gear and could be sent anywhere in the country very easily.

The Idea

There is a really great local (Utah) agency called Afton Klein Group that created incredible AR posters for an AIGA show that I had participated in. Seeing those posters in person made the whole event even more enjoyable. This experience sparked the idea that we could utilize the same technology to develop an AR packaging experience.

Kinetic type designed in After Effects.
Static image printed on the inside of the box.
Sunrun calls their sales people "Athletes" and uses sports themes to motivate and inspire.

The Results

I used After Effects to create create the cover of the box and went through a couple rounds of revisions for the physical design of it.

The AR packaging was a huge success with Sunrun management, and they felt it enhanced the onboarding experience for new salespeople joining the team. The AR elements functioned seamlessly and were incredibly entertaining to witness firsthand alongside the box.

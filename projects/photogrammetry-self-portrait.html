Photogrammetry Self-portrait
2021 — Personal Project, Photogrammetry, 3D Model, AR
Photogrammetry uses hundreds of photos to generate a 3D model. I am drawn to this process because it reminds me of screen printing. I used to screen print posters in my garage and each poster would be slightly different with built-in imperfections. In the same way, the 3D models produced from my photogrammetry process are often unique and full of imperfections.

This is my first photogrammetry portrait. With my features distorted, I love the uncanny valley that the portrait lives in. I also placed the model in an AR environment so I could share my portrait with everyone. Scan the QR code below to see the portrait in your space.

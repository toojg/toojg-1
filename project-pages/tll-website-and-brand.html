Tampico Lasting Love Website

Tampico Lasting Love

Tampico Lasting Love is an indie rock band whose music goes from chaos to melodic, while mixing strings, horns and digital programming.

The website needed to be a simple hub for links. Although simple, I wanted it to be playful, having the TLL logo follow the cursor around the screen.

See the website: tampicolastinglove.com
